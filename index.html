Backed by Science

Studies consistently demonstrate that proper warm-ups enhance performance and reduce injury risk.

Key Research Findings:

  • Meta-analysis of 32 studies found active warm-ups improved performance by 79% across all criteria (British Journal of Sports Medicine)
  • Neuromuscular warm-up programs reduced lower limb injuries by 33% and knee injuries by 52% (BMC Medicine, 2012)
  • Warm-ups increase oxygen dissociation, muscle blood flow, and nervous system activation (Sports Medicine Research)
  • Dynamic stretching as part of warm-ups activates 5.9-8.5% more quadricep muscle fibers (Journal of Sports Science and Medicine)
